# Demo Guide - Modern New Tab Extension

## 🎬 Quick Demo Script

Use this guide to demonstrate the extension's features.

## 1. First Impression (10 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Open new tab
- Show beautiful gradient background
- Point out clean, modern design
- Highlight the clock and date

**Script:**
> "This is your new tab page - beautiful, modern, and fully customizable."

---

## 2. Search Feature (15 seconds)

**What to show:**
1. Click search bar (or press `/`)
2. Show search engine dropdown
3. Select "ChatGPT"
4. Type "best productivity apps"
5. Hit Enter → Opens ChatGPT with query

**Script:**
> "Quick search with multiple engines - including ChatGPT for AI-powered answers. Just press slash to search."

---

## 3. Quick Links (20 seconds)

**What to show:**
1. Hover over existing links (Gmail, YouTube, etc.)
2. Show "×" remove button appears
3. Click "+ Add Site"
4. Add example: "Notion" → "notion.so" → "📝"
5. Show new link appears

**Script:**
> "Add your favorite sites with custom icons. Remove or rearrange anytime."

---

## 4. Quick Notes (15 seconds)

**What to show:**
1. Click 📝 icon on left toolbar
2. Notes panel slides in
3. Type some text
4. Close panel
5. Reopen → Show text is saved

**Script:**
> "Built-in notes that auto-save. Access from any new tab."

---

## 5. Weather Widget (10 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Point to weather widget (top right)
- Show current temperature and location
- Click 🌤️ to refresh

**Script:**
> "Live weather for your location - automatically detected."

---

## 6. Customization (25 seconds)

**What to show:**
1. Click ⚙️ Settings
2. Settings panel appears
3. Enter background URL: `https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4`
4. Click "Save"
5. Show background changes
6. Change clock format to 12-hour
7. Show clock updates

**Script:**
> "Fully customizable - add your own backgrounds, change clock format, and more."

---

## 7. Keyboard Shortcuts (5 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Press `/` anywhere
- Search bar gets focus

**Script:**
> "Keyboard shortcuts built-in - slash to search instantly."

---

## 8. Privacy & Performance (10 seconds)

**What to show:**
- Right-click extension icon → Inspect popup
- Show no network requests
- Show fast load time

**Script:**
> "Privacy-first design. No tracking, no analytics. Everything stays local."

## 💡 Demo Tips

### Do's ✅
- Show features quickly (people have short attention spans)
- Use visually appealing backgrounds
- Emphasize ChatGPT integration (unique selling point)
- Mention "no tracking" (privacy matters)
- Show keyboard shortcuts (power users love this)

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't spend too long on setup
- Don't show error states
- Don't demo on slow internet
- Don't use boring default gradient
- Don't skip the "wow" moments
